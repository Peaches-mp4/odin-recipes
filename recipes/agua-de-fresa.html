Mexican Strawberry Water (Agua de Fresa)

Description

Agua de fresa is a refreshing beverage made by blending fresh strawberries with water, sugar and sometimes milk.

Aguas frescas (or "fresh waters") are ice cold non-alcoholic drinks commonly sold by street vendors in Mexico and Central America. They are usually made by blending of fresh fruits, grains, seeds, nuts, herbs, spices and sweeteners.

According to the Mexican legends, Aztecs would add ice from the dormant volcanoes Popocatépetl and Iztaccíhuatl to make this drink cold!
